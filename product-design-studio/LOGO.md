## The paper-plane mark (rules from STYLE.md)

- **Wing:** the gradient blade `#62D38C` (nose) → `#30737B` (tail).
- **Body:** navy `#0C2A48` on light grounds; **white on navy**.
- **Never:** outlined, flat green, recolored, stretched, or rotated off its flight angle.
- **Background texture use:** faded oversized silhouettes at 6–10% opacity are an approved background treatment.

## Wordmark & lockup

- **Typeface:** Axiforma (Poppins fallback), per the brand type stack.
- **Lockup qualifier:** the logo lockup reads **"Product Design Studio"** — do not introduce new descriptor variants in lockups.
- **Nav tagline tracking:** 0.3em (`widestLg` token) — the widest tracking in the type system, reserved for the nav wordmark tagline.
- **Name rules:** always "This Waay" — two words, double-a; never "ThisWaay," "This Way," or "ThisWayUX."

## Misuse (derived from STYLE.md)

Never: outline the plane · fill it flat green · redraw the paths · add drop shadows beyond the standard card shadow system · place the raw green wing gradient on a green ground · pair with a second typeface.
