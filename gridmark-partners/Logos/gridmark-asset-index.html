Governance

Rules & changelog for the style guide

Clear space = one marked cell on all sides. Min sizes: full mark ≥ 40px wide; favicon cut below; never under 16px.
Color: navy on light · white on navy · all-brass on navy (premium print only). The marked cell is always brass — in every variant, every size.
Wordmark: Axiforma/Poppins · GRIDMARK 600 @ .14em · PARTNERS 400 @ 0.4× justified to name width · one hairline + one brass cell flush right.
Never: circle datum (retired) · flanking rules (retired) · non-brand colors · rounded terminals · the pre-v1.0 arch or lattice marks.
